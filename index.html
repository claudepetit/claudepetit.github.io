ABOUT

I'm professor of mathematics and statistician at INSEE. From 2021 to 2024 I was a visiting researcher at Inria, working with Sirocco / COMPACT team. In 2024-2025 I am with the DECIDE Team, affiliated with Lab-STICC, CNRS UMR 6285, University of Bretagne Sud (UBS).
